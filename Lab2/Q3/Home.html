Introduction

Machine Learning is a branch of Artificial Intelligence that focuses on building systems capable of learning and improving from experience without being explicitly programmed. It enables computers to analyze vast amounts of data and make predictions or decisions based on it.

Key Concepts

  • Supervised Learning
  • Unsupervised Learning
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Neural Networks
  • Feature Engineering

Applications

Machine Learning is used in various domains such as:

  • Healthcare: Predicting diseases and personalizing treatments
  • Finance: Fraud detection and algorithmic trading
  • Retail: Customer recommendations and inventory management
  • Autonomous Vehicles: Enhancing self-driving capabilities
